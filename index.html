Throughout the whole process, your money or goods will be kept by us, so you can be sure that they will not be transferred until the end of the arbitration. The decision is made only after consideration of all available facts. In some cases, specialized arbitrators may be involved.

Any transaction is made in several stages:

The customer contacts us in any convenient way, having previously agreed with the contractor to close the transaction through the garant-servis. He/she informs us about the transaction funds and the preferred payment system and provides data for communication with the contractor. Our guarantor provides an account for payment. It includes the amount to be transferred to the contractor, the interest of garant-servis (10%), and the commission fee of the payment system. After the receipt of funds to the account of garant-servis, we inform the contractor about it, and he/she can start providing his/her services or transferring goods. When the contractor provides his/her services, the confirmation of the customer is needed. The money is transferred to the contractor's account, and the transaction is considered to be completed.

Transaction rules:

If the contractor refused to close the transaction through the guarantor, the funds are returned to the customer. But what if the contractor provided poor-quality services or sold poor-quality goods? The funds, in this case, are returned to the customer only after the dispute resolution. The guarantor's remuneration is 10% of the amount.

Other conditions for closing transactions:

We are ready to act as a guarantor of transactions under other conditions, taking into consideration your wishes. However, in this case, the customer and the contractor will have to confirm all the conditions of the transaction. To avoid loss of time and money, the transfer of funds should be conducted in one payment system. If the transaction of funds is conducted in one payment system, the commission fee for the transfer is not charged. Depending on the complexity of the transaction, the guarantor can either increase or decrease the commission fee for the work. To avoid deception of one of the parties, all conditions should be agreed by them in advance. If it is not possible for the guarantor to check the quality of the goods or services, he/she may refuse to close the transaction. The money is credited to the seller's account through the exchange office. It usually takes 2-5 working days!
